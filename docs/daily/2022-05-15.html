Things I've Been Thinking About

I've been thinking recently about the value of hard things. I don't think difficulty is inherently correlated with value, but I often find myself doing things that are easy but not particularly important/valuable, and often end up getting a lot of mileage out of hard things (presumably because I'm too lazy to do anything that's hard but unimportant/not valuable). So I want to be more intentional about working on problems or subproblems that are hard but important
I also think there's something to be said for like "MIT is hard and valuable", but I don't think MIT is valuable because of its difficulty or really in spite of its difficulty
